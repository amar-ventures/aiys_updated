Yoga Teacher Training Programs

Transform your practice into a teaching journey with our comprehensive teacher training programs. Rooted in tradition, designed for the modern world.

200 Hours Beginner Friendly Yoga Alliance Certified

Level One: Foundation Program

Our foundational 200-hour program provides comprehensive training in yoga philosophy, teaching methodology, anatomy, and fundamental asanas. Designed for practitioners ready to begin their teaching journey or deepen their practice.

Program Highlights:

  • Asana alignment and teaching techniques
  • Yoga philosophy and history
  • Anatomy for yoga teachers

Available Formats:

  • 4-week intensive (Mon-Fri)
  • Weekend program (5 months)
  • Online hybrid (3 months)
300 Hours Advanced Yoga Alliance Certified

Level Two: Advanced Program

Take your teaching to the next level with our advanced 300-hour program. Deepen your knowledge of yoga philosophy, advanced asanas, specialized teaching methodologies, and therapeutic applications of yoga.

Program Highlights:

  • Advanced asana and adjustments
  • Therapeutic applications
  • Advanced teaching methodology

Prerequisites:

  • 200-hour certification
  • At least 1 year teaching experience
  • Regular personal practice
