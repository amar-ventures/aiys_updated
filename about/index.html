Our Story

Founded in 2010, Adishesha Institute emerged from a vision to create a sanctuary where ancient yoga wisdom meets modern understanding.

"We believe in preserving the essence of traditional yoga while making it accessible to contemporary practitioners."

Our journey began with a small group of dedicated practitioners and has grown into a globally recognized institution, training thousands of teachers who now share yoga's transformative power worldwide.

Our Mission

To nurture skilled yoga practitioners and teachers who honor the traditional roots of yoga while making its benefits accessible to all contemporary students.
